Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Heat up oil, saute paste ingredients to fragrant, add beef mince, cumin powder and black pepper powder.
  2. Transfer into bowl, cool it down. Break egg and mix it.
  3. In flat plate, add planta, pour the beef mixture and close it with french loaf.
  4. Flip it another side.
  5. Transfer into tray, Place the build ingredient into it
  6. Drizzle topping accordingly.
